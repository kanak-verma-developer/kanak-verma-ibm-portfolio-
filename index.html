The AI Virtual Interview (Mini Project) is an innovative solution designed to help students and professionals prepare for real-world interviews using artificial intelligence. It analyzes responses, communication style, and confidence level to provide constructive feedback. The system leverages natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning techniques to evaluate answers, identify strengths, and highlight areas for improvement. By offering a personalized and interactive experience, the project aims to reduce interview anxiety, enhance communication skills, and equip users with valuable insights for career success.
